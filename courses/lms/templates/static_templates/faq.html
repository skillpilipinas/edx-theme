Q. What do I do if I get an error saying my email address belongs to an existing account when trying to view a course?

This happens when you have already created an account on https://openedx.microsoft.com using your @microsoft.com email address. To resolve this, please:


  1. Sign-in to the https://openedx.microsoft.com site with your @microsoft.com email address.
  2. Follow the instructions on the "Account update required" page to sign in with or create a Microsoft account. (If you have a hotmail.com, live.com, or outlook.com email address, you already have a Microsoft account.)
  3. To complete the process, confirm your account update. At this point, you'll be able to sign in to https://openedx.microsoft.com site using your Microsoft account.
  4. You will then be able to register to the https://courses.microsoft.com site using your AAD credentials.
Note:Do note that the above mentioned steps will work only if you have learner role and don't have author permissions to sign-in to the CMS. Please contact the customer support team if the solution above does not work for you.

Q. How will I be notified of courses that are applicable to my role?

All Open edX courses are available on MyLearning, and if a course was developed for your role, you will find it under required or recommended training. You may also receive an email communication about certain campaigns.


Q. Will I be able to discover open edX courses on Infopedia?

Yes, the Open edX catalog will be available in Infopedia. If courses have not been required or recommended to you on MyLearning, you can locate them in the Infopedia catalog most easily with keyword search. Results improve if you filter on training and add MOOC to your query. Try these search examples:

"Microsoft SharePoint 2016" MOOC

"Microsoft Exchange Server 2016" MOOC

"Microsoft Project" MOOC

Q. What is the minimum percentage required to pass the course?

Each course can have a unique grading policy. Review the About page for the course to understand the requirement to pass the course.


Q. How do I track my course progress?

Open your course and select the Progress tab


Q. How do I register?

To register, click launch on a course card on Infopedia. You can register and enroll directly from Infopedia.


Q. I’d like to try the Open edX experience, but no courses are available to me right now.

The best way to get a sense of the user experience is by taking the edX Demo course. You can also find over 100 courses delivered by Microsoft that are open to the public on https://www.edx.org/school/microsoft.
